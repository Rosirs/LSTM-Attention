以下是最近五年与注意力机制相关的论文，按照APA格式整理：

1. Kim, J., Kim, T., Kim, S., & Yoo, C. D. (2019). Edge-labeling graph neural network for few-shot learning. In *2019 IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR)*, 11–20. 

2. Zhang, H., Goodfellow, I., Metaxas, D. N., & Odena, A. (2019). Self-attention generative adversarial networks. *International Conference on Machine Learning*, 7354–7363. 

3. Woo, S., Park, J., Lee, J. Y., & Kweon, I. S. (2018). CBAM: Convolutional block attention module. In *Computer Vision – ECCV 2018, Lecture Notes in Computer Science*, 3–19. 

4. Chen, Z., et al. (2023). Model for underwater acoustic target recognition with attention mechanism based on residual concatenate. *Journal of Marine Science and Engineering*, 12(1), 24. 

5. Xue, L., Zeng, X., & Jin, A. (2022). A novel deep-learning method with channel attention mechanism for underwater target recognition. *Sensors*, 22(15), 5492. 

6. Liu, C., Hong, F., & Feng, H., et al. (2021). Underwater acoustic target recognition based on dual attention networks and multiresolution convolutional neural networks. In *OCEANS 2021: San Diego-Porto*, 1–5. 

7. Jin, A., & Zeng, X. (2023). A novel deep learning method for underwater target recognition based on res-dense convolutional neural network with attention mechanism. *Journal of Marine Science and Engineering*, 11(1), 69. 

8. Chen, W., et al. (2020). Multi-range attentive bicomponent graph convolutional network for traffic forecasting. In *Proceedings of the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence*, 34, 3529–3536. 

9. Li, R., Zheng, S., Duan, C., Yang, Y., & Wang, X. (2020). Classification of hyperspectral image based on double-branch dual-attention mechanism network. *Remote Sensing*, 12, 582. 

10. Ma, W., Yang, Q., Wu, Y., Zhao, W., & Zhang, X. (2019). Double-branch multi-attention mechanism network for hyperspectral image classification. *Remote Sensing*, 11, 1307. 

这些论文涵盖了注意力机制在不同领域的应用，如图像分类、目标检测、自然语言处理、水下目标识别等，为该领域的研究提供了丰富的参考。
